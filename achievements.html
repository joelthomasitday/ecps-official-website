The Millennium World Peace Summit 2026

We are honored to announce our participation in the prestigious Millennium World Peace Summit 2026, a historic gathering that brings together the world's most influential religious and spiritual leaders at the United Nations. This landmark event represents a monumental step towards global peace and interfaith cooperation.

üåü A Historic Gathering

The Millennium World Peace Summit brings together one thousand of the world's leading religious and spiritual figures, including His Holiness Pope John Paul II, The Most Reverend Dr. George Carey, Archbishop of Canterbury, and other distinguished leaders from all major faith traditions.

üéØ Our Mission

Since the end of World War II, armed conflicts have claimed over 27 million lives worldwide, with 85% being civilian casualties. The Summit's goal is to create a forum where preeminent leaders of all world religions can pledge concrete actions for achieving world peace through the Declaration for World Peace.

üåç Global Impact

This unprecedented gathering establishes an International Advisory Council of Religious Leaders to function as a resource for the United Nations Secretary-General in resolving international conflicts, marking the first step toward developing a World Academy of Nonviolence and Ethics.

International Peace20 Youth Summit 2023

ECPS proudly participated in the International Peace20 Youth Summit 2023 held in Kochi, Kerala, South India. This transformative event brought together young leaders from around the world to champion peace, justice, and sustainable development.

üåü Youth Leadership for Peace

The Global Youth desire to tell world leaders that they do not want war, conflicts, intolerance, and tensions. They want Peace in Action, justice, and sustainable development, believing that peace and harmony is the only way humanity and the world can survive and co-exist peacefully.

üéØ Summit Highlights

The summit featured the 100-member Royal Philharmonic Orchestra performing India's National Anthem in London, youth ambassadors from Rajagiri Public School, Doha, and participants from around the world taking the Peace Pledge. The event emphasized the importance of youth leadership in achieving the UN Sustainable Development Goals.

üåç Global Youth Movement

International Peace20 Youth Summit welcomes organizations to be part of this Global Youth Movement for World Peace and Sustainable Development. The summit serves as a platform for young leaders to voice their commitment to creating a more peaceful and sustainable world.
