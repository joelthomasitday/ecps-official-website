ECPS

El Shaddai Center For Projects and Services

Empowering Countries With Special Needs through Education, Health, Technology, and Humanities - A Kochi-based organization dedicated to sustainable development through strategic partnerships and collaborations.

"LEAVING NO ONE BEHIND" – ALL INCLUSIVE

ECPS provides practical solutions in procurement and project management, working tirelessly to create positive change in communities worldwide.

Together with our partners, we support peace and security, humanitarian and development projects around the world – addressing urgent needs and helping shape a more sustainable, resilient and inclusive future for all.

ECPS associates with the UN to implement its Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) to uphold the motto "Peace and Prosperity for People and Planet" and also to conduct education, scientific and career seminars for high school & college students.

We provide comprehensive infrastructure, procurement and project management services for a sustainable world, ensuring that every project we undertake contributes to the betterment of society.
