Our Mission

LEAVING NO ONE BEHIND

NEPS provides practical solutions in procurement and project management, working tirelessly to create positive change in communities worldwide.

Together with our partners, we support peace and security, humanitarian and development projects around the world – addressing urgent needs and helping shape a more sustainable, resilient and inclusive future for all.

NEPS associates with the UN to implement its Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) to uphold the motto "Peace and Prosperity for People and Planet" and also to conduct education, scientific and career seminars.
