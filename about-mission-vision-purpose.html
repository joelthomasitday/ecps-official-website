Supporting LDCs

NEPS is committed to supporting the 46 countries currently designated by the United Nations as Least Developed Countries (LDCs). These nations face the most severe development challenges.

What are LDCs?

Countries characterized by low income, weak human assets, and high economic vulnerability.

Key Challenges

Extreme poverty, limited infrastructure, weak institutions, and high vulnerability.
