About Us

Welcome to ECPS (El Shaddai Center For Projects and Services), a dedicated organization committed to sustainable development and humanitarian assistance. We work tirelessly to implement the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) and create positive change in communities worldwide.

Our Mission

MOBILIZING international support for implementation of the UN SDG 2030 and PFF (Pact for the Future) adopted by the UN General Assembly in 2024.

INVITING partners to implement and support the Global Development Agenda on SDGs and PFF.

Our Vision

World Peace, Sustainable Development, Human Rights & Environment.

Our Focus

PEOPLE, PLANET, PEACE & PROSPERITY
